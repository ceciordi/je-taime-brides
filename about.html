Meet the artists

Cecilia is an international freelance hairstylist, born and raised in Paris, living in New York City. She is passionate about creating beautiful bridal hairstyles with a modern approach. Cecilia started her career assisting star hairdressers such as Guido, Eugene Souleiman and Orlando Pita and worked on all most significant shows of NY, Milan and Paris. Spent a number of years in Los Angeles working with a celebrity clientèle including Angelina Jolie, Fiona Apple, Jim Carrey, Diana Ross and Rosario Dawson. With 14 years of experience, her strength is in putting the hair up, doing up-dos, chignons, super clean pony tails and buns; from old school French hairstyling to pretty, loose waves to 1920’s fingers waves to haircutting and barbering, all with a cool, effortless style.
